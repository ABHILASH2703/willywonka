About Us

The history of chocolates & cakes goes back to the 17th century, when cocoa powder from the Americas was added to traditional cake recipes. However, it was not until the 19th century that chocolate cake became widely available and affordable. The chocolates & cakes in the US were made by adding chocolate to the cake batter, rather than using it only for fillings and glazes. These cakes were moist, rich, and fudgy, and soon became a favorite for special occasions.
